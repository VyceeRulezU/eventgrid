# Aftermath & Reports

**Route:** `/events/:id/aftermath`

---

## The Big Picture

The event is over. Now you need to:
- **Document what happened**
- **Show the client a report**
- **Learn for next time**

The Aftermath page is your post-event hub.

---

## Report Builder

Generate a professional **PDF report** of the event.

### What's in the Report
- **Event overview** — name, date, venue, type
- **Vendor list** — all vendors with costs
- **Budget summary** — planned vs actual spending
- **Guest count** — invited, confirmed, attended
- **Notes** — anything you want to add

### Generating a Report
1. Click **Build Report**
2. Select what to include (vendors, budget, guests, etc.)
3. Click **Generate PDF**
4. The PDF downloads directly to your device

---

## Lessons Learned

Document what went well and what could be better:
- **What worked** — things to repeat next time
- **What didn't** — things to improve
- **Surprises** — unexpected things that happened

This section is for **you and your team** to reference for future events.

### Adding a Lesson
1. Click **Add Lesson**
2. Choose: **What went well** or **What could improve**
3. Write your note
4. Click **Save**

---

## Media Gallery

Upload photos from the event:
1. Click **Upload Media**
2. Select photos from your device
3. They appear in a gallery grid

### Organising
- Photos are grouped by upload date
- Click a photo to view full size

---

## What Coordinators See

Coordinators can **view** the aftermath page but cannot generate reports or add lessons/media.

---

## Pro Tips
- **Generate the report** within a week while details are fresh
- Add **lessons learned** right away so you don't forget
- Upload **candid photos** for client memories
- The PDF report is great to include in your **portfolio**
