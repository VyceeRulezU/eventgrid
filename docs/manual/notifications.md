# Notifications & Feedback

**Route:** `/notifications` (notifications), Sidebar (feedback)

---

## The Big Picture

You need to know when:
- Someone replies to your **feedback**
- A **task** is assigned to you
- A **payment** is confirmed
- Your **client portal** is accessed

All of this shows up in your Notifications.

---

## Notifications Page (`/notifications`)

### What You'll See
A list of all your notifications, newest first:
- **Unread** items are highlighted with a coloured border
- **Read** items are dimmed

### Marking as Read
- Click a notification to mark it **read**
- Click **Mark All Read** at the top to clear everything at once

### Notification Types
| Type | Why you get it |
|------|----------------|
| Feedback reply | An admin replied to your feedback |
| Task assigned | Someone assigned you a task |
| Payment received | A client payment went through |
| Portal access | A client viewed their portal |

---

## Feedback

Have a suggestion or found a bug? Use the **Feedback** button in the sidebar (bottom-left corner).

### Submitting Feedback
1. Click the **Feedback** button (message icon) in the sidebar
2. Choose a category:
   - **Bug Report** — something isn't working
   - **Feature Request** — something you want added
   - **General** — anything else
3. Write your message
4. Click **Send**

### What Happens Next
- A super admin sees your feedback
- They can reply to you
- When they reply, you get a **notification**
- Go to `/notifications` to see their response

---

## What Coordinators See

Coordinators have the same notification and feedback access as planners.

---

## Pro Tips
- **Report bugs** as soon as you find them so they can be fixed
- Check your **notifications** at the start of each day
- Use feedback for **feature ideas** — they might get built!
