# Team Management

**Route:** `/events/:id/team`

---

## The Big Picture

You can't run an event alone. You need coordinators, assistants, and staff. The Team page lets you:
1. **Invite people** to your organisation
2. **Assign roles** (coordinator, staff)
3. **See who's on your team**

---

## Your Organisation Team

When you create an account, you get an organisation. Everyone you invite joins that org.

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **View team** | Table of all members in your org |
| **Invite member** | Enter their email and select a role |
| **Remove member** | Click the remove button (cannot remove yourself) |

### Roles
| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Planner** | Full access — manage everything |
| **Coordinator** | Can view and update tasks, live board, guests (check-in only), team |
| **Staff** | Limited access — assigned tasks only |

---

## Inviting Someone

1. Click **Invite Member**
2. Enter their **email address**
3. Select their **role** (Planner or Coordinator)
4. Click **Send Invite**

An email is sent via Resend with a join link. When they accept, they appear in the team list.

### Invite Status
- **Pending** — invite sent, not yet accepted
- **Accepted** — they joined

---

## What Coordinators See

Coordinators can view the team list but cannot invite or remove members.

---

## Pro Tips
- Invite coordinators **early** so they can help plan
- Check invite status — if it's pending for too long, resend
- You can have multiple planners in one org
